RIP AirDrop

3 Dec, 2022 - 3 minutes
#blog
#airdrop
#apple
According to this article from CNBC, Apple will be changing how AirDrop works. Originally, AirDrop had three options: Receiving off Contacts Only Everyone Having the third option selected is pretty self explanatory, it allowed you to received messages from anyone. But now they’re going to be changing that third option to “Everyone (For 10 Minutes)” This means that AirDrop will be enabled to receive messages from anyone for only 10 minutes at a time.
